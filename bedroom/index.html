Bedroom furniture

The Collection

Coordinated sets for the modern bedroom

Our bedroom collection is designed to work together. Beds, nightstands, dressers and storage share finishes and proportions, so a customer can buy a full suite or add pieces over time.

Finishes range from clean light high-gloss to contemporary upholstered designs — the looks New York shoppers ask for most.

  • Full sets or individual pieces
  • Standard US bed sizes
  • Coordinated finishes across the range
