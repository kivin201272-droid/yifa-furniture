Living room furniture

Modern seating and occasional furniture — the sofas, chairs and tables that anchor a contemporary living space.

The Collection

Seating that works hard and looks current

Our living room collection focuses on the pieces customers replace most often: comfortable, well-built sofas and the occasional furniture that surrounds them.

Upholstery options span fabric and leather looks in modern tones, while frames keep to clean, contemporary lines that suit a wide range of interiors.

  • Fabric and leather-look upholstery
  • Modern, clean-lined frames
  • Mix-and-match seating and tables
